So You Want to Go to Paris?

Read this guide first. Here are the basics of what you need for your first trip to Paris.

Tips to get you started

  1. Money! Make sure you save money to enjoy yourself. This city is not cheap.
  2. Learn some French from some youtube videos or buy a French phrasebook. It will make your experience exploring the city so much sweeter.
  3. Search for discounted tickets! This can save you quite a bit if you are diligent.
  4. Check out airbnb for more reasonable accommodations.
  5. Pack light! Check out youtube videos on how to pack a carryon bag for a trip to Paris for one month. This will forever change the way you pack for anything.

Right now you may be wondering, what should I pack? The weather in Paris is generally pleasant year round but you can't go wrong with these essentials:

  • a carryon size suitcase that has both straps to be a backback and wheels to roll. Just trust me on this one.
  • waterproof shoes that are also good walking shoes. Again, trust me. LOL
  • an unbrella
  • packing cubes
  • a week's worth of clothes that are easy to wash.
